Stocks

In this webpage there are options to navigate with audio!


Make sure your mic is enabled to use this feature.

  • "Hello" - Sends Alert saying "Hello World"
    • Say: Hello
  • "Change color to [color]" - Changes background color
    • Say: Change color to *color
  • "Navigate to [page_name] - Navigates to Home, Stocks, or Dogs page"
    • Say: Navigate to *page_name
